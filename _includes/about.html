About

Old People Soccer is a casual coed pickup soccer game in Falls Church. It is pretty low-key, and open to anyone. We try to play each weekend. It's a great way to have fun and get some exercise.

The participants are from the Falls Church area, about 35-50 years old, mostly parents (some of the kids play too). The attitude is casual - we don't generally keep score, aiming to avoid injury rather than win.
